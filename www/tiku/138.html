The word " twin" in the first sentence means_______.

  • A

    a brother or sister of step mother or father

  • B

    either of two children born together of the same mother

  • C

    an adopted child

  • D

    a deserted child

    • 查看正确答案

    正确答案：B

    本词汇题的关键是确认这个单词在文章中出现的地方（第1个句子），再找到上下文的词义线索“…the two planets are so like each other in size”，两者很相似，符合“双胞胎”的特点。
